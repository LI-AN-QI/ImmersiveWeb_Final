The project draws inspiration from Mayday's Nora's Ark released in 2011, a poignant reflective piece triggered by the Wenchuan earthquake and other catastrophic events. Reflections on the subtle line between the human world and potential doom are the thematic basis of this work.

From the park bench where the two souls first met, to the goodbyes in the last phone call. From all the wealth we have ever possessed, to all the fame, relationships, and status we have ever held.

How insignificant every precious memory, regrettable emotion and longed-for hope seems against the doomsday.

If the day comes when tomorrow never comes and forever becomes a distance, how will we say goodbye? How would we find the courage to say goodbye?
